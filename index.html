This is Zander.

Zander is a big-hearted but naive, shaped by his magical bloodline and the weight of his kingdom’s survival. Driven by loyalty and what might be love, he navigates a world of sacrifice, rivalry, and destiny with both strength and vulnerability, making him a hero you can’t help but root for.
